About Us

HT Green was established in 1997. We specialise in green technologies such as solar, wind turbines, geothermal and now small-scale hydro plants.

Company Directors Robert and Janet Wilkins built HT Green from the ground up with a small business grant and big dreams to power homes and businesses with energy derived from natural sources.

At HT Green Energy Consultants, our unwavering commitment to a sustainable future drives our every endeavor. Founded on the principle that responsible energy practices are not just a choice, but a necessity, we have emerged as a leading force in the renewable energy sector. With a deep-rooted passion for environmental stewardship and a team of experts who are pioneers in their respective fields, we are dedicated to providing innovative solutions that empower individuals and organizations to transition to cleaner, more efficient energy sources.

Our journey began with a vision to reshape the energy landscape, reducing carbon footprints and promoting sustainable practices. Today, we proudly stand at the forefront of the green energy revolution. Our comprehensive suite of services encompasses every aspect of renewable energy, from initial assessments and feasibility studies to design, installation, and ongoing maintenance. Whether you are an individual homeowner seeking to harness the power of the sun through solar panels, a commercial entity exploring energy-efficient solutions, or a community striving for energy independence, HT Green Energy Consultants is your trusted partner.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to excellence, innovation, and personalized service. We understand that each project is unique, and we approach every challenge with a tailor-made strategy. Our team of experienced professionals collaborates closely with clients, leveraging their expertise to craft sustainable energy solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and cost-effectiveness ensures that you receive the best value for your investment.
